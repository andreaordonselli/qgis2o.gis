about=
 QGIS plugin to make Lite version of OpenGIS, from your current QGIS project.
 
 Made with OpenLayers, it replicates as many aspects of the project as it can, including layers, styles (categorized, graduated and svg icon) and other. No server-side software required.
 
 Demo Lite version at https://opengis.it/lite-version/
 
 Demo Full version at https://opengis.it/demo
 
 Born from the qgis2web v.3.16.0 repository, special thanks go to the creators of qgis2web: Tom Chadwin, Riccardo Klinger, Victor Olaya, Nyall Dawson and to all the users of github.com, stackoverflow.com and gis.stackexchange.com
